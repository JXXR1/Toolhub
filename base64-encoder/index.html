Base64 Encoder/Decoder

Encode and decode text to/from Base64 with our free online tool. Easy to use for developers and web professionals.

How to Use

  1. Enter or paste your text in the input field.
  2. Select 'Encode' to convert text to Base64, or 'Decode' to revert it back.
